Hotel MUA

Our Hotel MUA is in a superb location with friendly and helpful staff. The hostel offers comfortable, clean rooms and fantastic access to Kandy's most famous sights and attractions.

Hotel RIU

The RIU Hotel comprises five interconnected Victorian era buildings to form a 103 room hotel. All the rooms have been furnished with a blend of style and functionality in mind. Simple painted walls mean rooms feel light, while surfaces feature a dark wood veneer, such as bedheads, shelves and wardrobes. Each room has an en suite shower room tucked behind a white wooden door. 

Cinnamon
